How I Met Your Mother


Introduction

How I Met Your Mother or HIMYM is a sitcom that aired from September 19, 2005 to March 31, 2014. The 9 season series follows the story of Ted Mosby telling his kids stories in the year 2030 about meeting the kids' mom. During the story the dad tells a lot of side stories (9 years worth) leading up to revealing the moment when he met the kids' mother. Craig Thomas and Carter Bays created this show. Over the course of its run the show won nine Emmy Awards and Hannigan and Harris won People's Choice Award for Favorite TV Comedy Actress and Actor respectively. The show was nominated for a total of 72 awards and won 18 during it's 9 season run. The first seasons got good reviews but the last season received mixed reviews due to the series finale's surprise ending.

Brief Summary

Through HIMYM's 9 seasons Ted has many relationships with girls in New York City, but ultimately breaks them all off in an attempt to date Robin (one of his best friends). Robin eventually ends up with Barney (another of Ted's best friends). The show skims over the awkardness of the Ted/Barney/Robin friendship. Lily and Marshall are the stable couple of the group and provide a stark contrast to Barney who seduces a different girl every night.

The whole show is Ted telling a story so it is one big flash back, but each episode flashbacks within the overarching flashbacks. During each episode the directors twisted the timeline of the flashbacks creating new ways to tell stories. This way of story telling kept the show interesting and refreshing.
